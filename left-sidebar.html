Oatmeal and Blueberries Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • ½ cup oats
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 4-5 pitted dates
  • ½ tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch cinnamon
  • 3-5 cubes ice

This smoothie is high in fiber, low in fat and vegan-friendly.

Start the day on the right foot with this protein packed blueberry oatmeal smoothie. Loaded with dietary fiber, the oatmeal will help you feel full for longer. Sweetened with blueberries, almond milk, vanilla extract and dates, it is a great way to begin your day. And it’s vegan-friendly!

Instructions

Add all ingredients in a blender and blend for about one minute. Add additional milk as needed to reach desired consistency. Serve and enjoy!

Preperation time: 5 minutes.
Number of servings: 1.

About

Coming from the South-East of Europe, where agriculture is flourishing due to the fertile land, Mediterranean climate and longer hours of sunshine in general, I have been lucky to grow up eating abundance of fresh fruit, vegetables, nuts and grains. Healthy food has always been inevitable part of my diet and this is partly down to the fact that I lived with my retired grandparents who produced and looked after a variety of organic crops in the backyard of our house. Every year, I remember how excited I was for the summer to finally arrive, meaning my favourite sweet strawberries, succulent peaches and grapes, wholesome almonds and sunflower seeds would finally become ripe and ready to be picked. I enjoyed experimenting and trying out different meals with them, mixing and matching ingredients, melding all flavours into all sorts of delicious combinations.

This leads me to introducing Superfood Smoothies which is the culmination of my best tried and tested creations over the years. The 10 hand-picked recipes are super easy, quick to prepare and can be consumed on a daily basis. The wide variety of fruit, vegetables and superfoods, and the mixture of paleo, vegan, dairy-free, and many more guarantee a favourite smoothie for everyone.

Hope you enjoy my smoothies and find inspiration to create mixtures of your own favourite foods. Let’s get blending!
